\englishabstract{We consider possibility of decoding music features from EEG signal. We utilize datasets of joint music and EEG recordings. First we ask whether song name can be predicted [and get a mixed answer]. We explain the result with a DTW experiment. We further ask whether music features can be predicted. For that we classify eeg snippet into "slow" and "fast" based on tempo of related music snippet.}
